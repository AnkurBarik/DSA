Question 1: Recall the Importance of Ayurveda in the Indian Knowledge System (IKS).

Ayurveda, an ancient Indian system of medicine, holds a significant place in the Indian Knowledge System (IKS). It is a holistic approach to health and wellness that focuses on balance between the body, mind, and spirit. The importance of Ayurveda in IKS includes:
	1.	Historical Significance – Ayurveda dates back over 5,000 years and is documented in classical texts like the Charaka Samhita and Sushruta Samhita.
	2.	Holistic Health Approach – It emphasizes prevention rather than cure, promoting a balanced lifestyle, diet, yoga, and meditation.
	3.	Natural Healing – Ayurveda uses natural herbs, minerals, and therapies for healing, reducing dependency on synthetic drugs.
	4.	Integration with Modern Science – Many Ayurvedic principles align with modern health concepts, and research continues to validate its effectiveness.
	5.	Global Influence – Ayurveda is now recognized worldwide as an alternative medical system, contributing to global healthcare.

Question 2: Describe the Indian Knowledge System (IKS).

The Indian Knowledge System (IKS) is a vast, interdisciplinary framework of traditional knowledge that has evolved over centuries. It encompasses various domains of science, philosophy, art, and technology.
	1.	Vedic and Philosophical Foundations – Rooted in the Vedas, Upanishads, and ancient scriptures, IKS includes diverse subjects like Ayurveda, Yoga, Vedanta, and Nyaya.
	2.	Science and Mathematics – India contributed significantly to fields like astronomy (Jyotish Shastra), metallurgy, and mathematics (concept of zero, decimal system).
	3.	Medicine and Health – Ayurveda and Siddha systems provide a deep understanding of holistic healthcare.
	4.	Linguistics and Literature – Sanskrit and Tamil texts preserve extensive knowledge in linguistics, poetry, and grammar.
	5.	Architecture and Engineering – Ancient Indian architecture (Vastu Shastra) and water management systems show advanced engineering skills.
	6.	Environmental Awareness – IKS promotes sustainable living through ecological balance, Ayurveda, and traditional farming methods.

Overall, IKS is a treasure trove of wisdom that continues to inspire modern advancements in science, medicine, and philosophy.
